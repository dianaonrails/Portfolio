OnSales is an windows app for a pharmaceutical company, where the employees can manage and interact with all the areas of the company.

Together we did visual explorations and created a platform where, the employees would not feel frustrated but could be faster and happier.





View Clients

The list of clients has a category associated, the location and a owner. It has two possible views grid and list.




View Visits

There is a list of visits scheduled of clients where the agents need to go during the year. It has two possible views grid and list.





Schedule Visits - Step 1

In the Agenda section we can schedule visits, first we choose a free day.


Schedule Visits - Step 2

Then we need to select the clients to visit, which appear in the right side of the screen.


Schedule Visits - Step 3

In the step 3, we need to add the time and other specifications like the type and nature of the visit.
