I was faced with the opportunity to create a platform that can bring together hotels and the local business to provide great experiences to the guests. The business owners could register and submit their experience plan, after approval the clients could choose an experience.




Registration

A potential partner(business owner) can register in the platform in 3 easy steps.




CREATE A PROGRAM

The partner can create and submit programs, that will be evaluated by the hotel







SEE BOOKINGS

After the program is accepted and starts having clientes, the partner will be able to manage bookings and budget in the platform







EVALUATE PROGRAMS

The list of clients has a category associated, the location and a owner. It has two possible views grid and list.









BOOK AN EXPERIENCE

The guest of the hotel can search at any time experiences to do during their stay.
